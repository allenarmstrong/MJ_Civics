Citizenship Objectives

After completing this unit, you will be able to:

  • print and use the Citizenship Checklist
  • complete the Citizenship Pretest
  • use a map to locate the 50 U.S. states and capital cities
  • use a map to locate U.S. territories and protectorates
  • locate major physical U.S. landmarks
  • define culture
  • identify major cultural regions of North America
  • locate major U.S. cultural landmarks
  • interpret maps to identify political boundaries
  • use GIS technology to study current information about the United States
  • define ecology and conservation
  • use a map to study issues of conservation or ecology
  • describe how citizenship and ecology are connected
  • define "citizen"
  • identify paths to U.S. citizenship
  • identify and evaluate duties and responsibilities of citizens
  • experience citizen responsibilities
    • conduct an interview (Advanced)
    • identify different types of service (Advanced)
  • review the major concepts and terms from the module
  • complete a Discussion-Based Assessment with your instructor
