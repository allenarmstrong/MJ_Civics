Objectives

Like Erik, everyone has rules to follow. You may have had an experience like Erik where a rule was not clear. Erik and his mother interpreted her rule in different ways. We have rules and lawsLaws: a rule established by government or other source of authority to regulate people's conduct or activities to follow to make life safer and better. Let's learn more about where rules and laws come from and why we have them.

Objectives

  • identify sources and types of law
