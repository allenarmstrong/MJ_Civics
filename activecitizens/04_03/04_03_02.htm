Objectives

Throughout American history, active citizens have worked together to make changes to improve people's lives. Often, the talents of artists, musicians, and writers can help bring greater attention to an issue. Art can inspire more people to participate in actions that might lead to a change in public policyPublic policy: a course of action accepted or used by a government to respond to a problem. Let's learn more about the influence of art on citizens.
