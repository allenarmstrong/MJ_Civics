Bailey knows that service to the community feels good and makes a positive change. In 2008, more than 1 million American students gave nearly 20 million hours to their communities. In this lesson, you will discover how you too can make a difference as you work to plan and complete your service project. You can learn more about Bailey by visiting the Hero Hugs website at http://www.herohugs.org.





Use this CAP File file to make notes during this lesson.



Objectives

After completing this lesson, you will be able to:

  • explain why service is valuable to a community
  • identify the steps of conducting a service project
  • conduct a service project to further the public good
  • reflect on and celebrate the service experience
