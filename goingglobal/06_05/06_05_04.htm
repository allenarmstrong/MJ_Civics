What Are Examples of Each Policy Type?

The United States faces a variety of issues each day. Some relate to foreign policy, while some are domestic. The issues change over time. People's attitudes and world events shape what they find important. Select issues in this image for descriptions of current policy issues.

Are you interested in global issues? You can work together with your fellow online students to find ways to help solve a global issue. Select the Collaboration Option for details on using this activity to meet your collaboration requirement.
