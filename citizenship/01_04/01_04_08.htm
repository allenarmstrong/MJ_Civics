What Can I Do?


© 2011 Comstock/Thinkstock

You can conserve and protect with many daily actions. You can shut off the water while brushing your teeth. You can plant a tree. You can create useful items from scrap material. You can donate clothing you have outgrown to charity. All of these efforts help the environment.

Collective action can have an even greater impact. Groups like Tampa Bay Watch raise money and organize people to make change in a large area. They work with government leaders to pass laws that aid their work.




Find out if there are groups like Tampa Bay Watch in your area. Learn how you can help and share the information with others. Select the Collaboration Option for details on using this activity to meet your collaboration requirement.
