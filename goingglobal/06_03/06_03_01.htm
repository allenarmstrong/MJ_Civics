Which Store Would You Visit?

Imagine you only have enough time to visit one of these candy shops. Examine the storefronts to determine the more appealing shop.

You must be a gum fanatic! Most students choose Candy Explosion. The reason is that not only does it have gumballs, but dozens of other candies.
You must be a fan of all types of candy! Most other students also choose Candy Explosion. The reason is that not only does it have gumballs, but dozens of other candies.
