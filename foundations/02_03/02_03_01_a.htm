Letter Text Version

Dear Meg,

I know we have been best friends for like a bazillion years or so, but it has been tough lately. I remember playing in the sandbox with you in pre-K. We have been to all of each other's birthday parties. I love you like my sister, and this is so difficult for me to write.

Yet the past few months you have ignored my feelings and our friendship. You did not come to even one of my bowling matches, but I went to all of your softball games. You ignore my texts and pretend you do not see me in public. These things are hurtful to me. When I saw your party invite with a note telling me what I should buy for you, I knew I had had enough.

I can't stay friends with someone who makes me feel so bad. I am sorry, Meg, but from now on I will no longer return your messages, attend your parties, or cheer at your games. I will focus on hanging out with friends who care about me.

Take care,

Samuel
