Celebrate!


© 2011 AP Images

After reflecting on your service, you should celebrate the project by sharing it with others. Let others celebrate and learn from your experience, too!

You will make a presentation about your service project. The format for your presentation is your choice. Think about what you can do to make it fun and how you can share it with your classmates!





Remember, you will need at least one photograph of you in action for your presentation. Be sure to smile!
