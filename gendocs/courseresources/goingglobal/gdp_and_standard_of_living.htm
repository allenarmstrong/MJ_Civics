GDP and Standard of Living

Gross Domestic Product (GDP)
  • GDP is the market value of all final goods and services produced within a country in a given period.
Crime Rate
  • A crime rate is the number of crimes committed per 10,000 people for a specific area, like a city or country.
Poverty Rate
  • A poverty rate is the portion of the population that earns less than a certain amount of income.
Literacy Rate
  • A literacy rate is the portion of the population that can read and write in an area.
Average Life Expectancy
  • The average life expectancy is the number of years a person born now can expect to live in a certain area.
Internet Users
  • Internet users are the number of people that have access and the knowledge required to use the Internet.
