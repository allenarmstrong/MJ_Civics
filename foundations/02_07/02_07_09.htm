What Do I Have to Do?

You should now be a Bill of Rights master! In this assignment, you will show that you are familiar with the protections and limits of the rights contained in the Bill of Rights.

Steps

Â© 2011 AP Images
  1. Review your CAP file. Make sure you have a list of rights from the lesson listed in the file.
  2. Circle or highlight the right that you think is most important.
  3. Write a paragraph that explains why you think this right is most important. Any choice of right is correct, but you must use information from the lesson to explain your choice.
  4. Write a second paragraph to explain how this right is protected and how you think it might be limited.
  5. Read the Discussion Posting Guidelines. Then post your two paragraphs to the discussion area.
  6. Respond to at least two other students' posts. Tell them why you agree with their explanations or what you would add. Use information from the lesson in your posts.

Please view the Rights Grading Rubric before starting the assignment.

  1. Complete the reading and activities for this lesson.
  2. Review your CAP file for this lesson.
  3. Complete and submit your Discussion Posts to Foundations 07 How Are Rights Protected?
