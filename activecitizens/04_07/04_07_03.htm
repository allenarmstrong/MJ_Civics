What Happens During an Election?

Prior to an election, candidates announce that they are running for office and begin their campaigns. Many events take place, and the mediaMedia: the various means of mass communication as a whole group, such as television, radio, newspapers, magazines, and the Internet cover them. Voters make sure they are registered. They learn about the candidates and consider their own beliefs about government before determining their vote.

Complete this Concord City election simulation to experience the election process and its effects on a community. Be sure to examine all parts of the city carefully before voting.
