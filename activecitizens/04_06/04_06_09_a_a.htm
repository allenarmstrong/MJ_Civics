Terms: Answers Text Version

Instructions: Provide the term for the following definitions.

  1. Preference toward a particular belief or attitude–Bias
  2. A group of people with shared goals who work together to influence public policy–Interest group
  3. The various means of mass communication as a whole group, such as television, radio, newspapers, magazines, and the Internet–Media
  4. To watch over somebody or something–Monitor
  5. A person chosen to speak and act for their fellow citizens in government–Representative
  6. A person or group guarding against illegal, wasteful, or unacceptable practices–Watchdog
  7. A course of action accepted or used by a government to respond to a problem–Public policy
  8. The right of the president and other officials in the executive branch to withhold certain information from Congress or the courts–Executive privilege
  9. The president must obey a court order to release information.–United States v. Nixon
