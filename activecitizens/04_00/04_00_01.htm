What Are Active Citizens?

Active citizensCitizen: a person who is native or naturalized to a country are people who are serious about their civic responsibilityCivic responsibility: obligations of citizens to their community. They learn how government works and stay informed about the actions it takes. Sometimes they challenge limits on their rights. They vote when they are eligible and some run for office themselves. Active citizens care about their communitiesCommunity: a group of people who live in the same area, have a common background, or share similar interests and the people living in them. They find ways to help others.
