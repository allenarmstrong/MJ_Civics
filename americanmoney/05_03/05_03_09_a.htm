Match the Cause and Effect

Instructions: Match the cause to the correct effect.

Cause

  • An inventor obtains a patent for a new invention.
  • A federal court rules that a certain company is violating antitrust law.
  • A regulation sets a minimum price for a certain product.

Effect

  • Companies will have to obtain permission and give payment to the inventor to produce and sell the invention.
  • Businesses that sell the item change their prices to reflect the new regulation. Some have to increase their prices and lose customers. Those who lower their prices might gain customers.
  • The company that has a monopoly must split into separate companies or send customers to a different company.
