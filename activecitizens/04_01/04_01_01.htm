How Could Your Community Better Serve Teens?

A county council organized a meeting of local teens. The county has funds available to promote activities for young teens in the area. Council members asked this panel of teens to discuss and recommend the best ways to use the funds. Listen to their conversation.

Please click on the 'Begin' button to start the slide show

We could build a new playground. The ones we have are old and meant for small children.

Most people our age are done with playgrounds, though. We need something with an older feel, like a skate park.

I think it needs to be something along the bus route, so teens can access it. The downtown area doesn't have room for a skate park. What do you think about expanding the teen section of the public library?

We do plenty of reading for school, thanks. We could just donate the money to the recreation center. The money could help the center buy new sports equipment and offer some classes, like chess.

I think the council wants us to come up with something new. Maybe we should survey more teens in our county for ideas.
