Recap Quiz Text Version

  1. A government where one person makes all of the decisions would be an
    1. oligarchy
    2. democracy
    3. autocracy
    4. unitary
  2. Of the following countries, which trades the most with the United States?
    1. Mexico
    2. Ghana
    3. France
    4. China
  3. Decisions made about war in another country are part of
    1. domestic policy
    2. national intervention
    3. international intervention
    4. foreign policy
  4. Which of the following groups is least likely to depend daily on currency exchange?
    1. teachers
    2. importers
    3. exporters
    4. travelers
  5. The United States, Canada, and Mexico are all
    1. confederations
    2. federations
    3. unitary governments
    4. oligarchy governments
  6. What is a challenge for a country that depends on a single resource or product?
    1. If something happens to its main resource, the country has no other means to trade or support itself.
    2. The country has too many countries that wish to buy its goods and services.
    3. The country will have a much greater amount of exports than it will import.
    4. If other countries have the same resource, then no one will want to trade with that country.
  7. Providing education and health care to U.S. citizens is part of
    1. domestic policy
    2. foreign policy
    3. Department of State
    4. national diplomacy
  8. Which of the following conflicts is an example of how the United States has used almost every foreign policy position?
    1. Korean War
    2. Spanish-American War
    3. War in Afghanistan
    4. World War Two
  9. Why is using "per capita" data important for comparing countries?
    1. It limits the number of data you use, so it is easier to compare countries.
    2. It tells you which data are available for the different countries you want to compare.
    3. It divides information by population, so you get a more accurate understanding.
    4. It only tells you the data for the capital city of the country so the type of area is the same.
  10. All of the following are important natural resources in North America, except
    1. diamonds
    2. oil
    3. coal
    4. gold
  11. Who would be happy if the United States has a strong dollar?
    1. a U.S. exporter of American goods
    2. a U.S. importer of Mexican goods
    3. a U.S. exporter of Mexican goods
    4. a U.S. importer of American goods
  12. Which of the following international organizations aims to use diplomacy to prevent world war in the future?
    1. United Nations, or UN
    2. North Atlantic Treaty Organization, or NATO
    3. Red Cross
    4. World Trade Organization, or WTO
