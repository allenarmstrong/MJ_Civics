Ecological Issue Text Version

Problems from an oil spill:

  1. Oil well explodes, causing leak.
  2. Oil pollutes water, destroys plant and animal life
  3. Fishing businesses close, people out of work.
  4. Other industries affected, like scientific research and travel
  5. Families have trouble paying bills, concerns about the health and future of their communities
  6. People demand government action
  7. Government sends money to pay for cleanup and to help people
  8. Government must find sources of money to pay for the cleanup
  9. The company owning the well helps pay, people pay through taxes and higher prices for affected items like oil and fish.
