Jackie's Move Text Version

Hello, I'm Jackie! Last year I moved to Florida from another state. It was difficult to leave my friends, but I have made new ones here. I keep in touch online with friends from my old hometown.

Though both states are part of the United States, they have many differences. The weather is different, but so are the laws.

For example, the laws about riding bicycles are different. I used to ride my bicycle all the time in my hometown. In my new town, I have to be home by a curfew and must wear a helmet!

I never had to wear a helmet in my old town. When I told that to a local officer, he said I have to follow the rules here and buy myself a helmet. I understand it's for safety, but, well, they just don't look cool. I made mine better, though, with glitter glue and stickers. I even made friends when people started asking me to decorate their helmets!

Before I moved, all my concern was about leaving my friends and making new ones. Now I realize that differences between U.S. states can create other changes in people's lives when they move. I had no idea!
