Government of the State of Florida and Comparisons to the National Government

Legislative Branch—makes the laws

The Florida Legislative has two houses—like the U.S. Congress
Florida Senate—like the U.S. Senate
Florida House of Representatives—like the U.S. House of Representatives

Executive Branch—enforces the laws

Governor—like the U.S. President
Lieutenant Governor—like the U.S. Vice President

Judicial Branch—interprets the laws

Florida Supreme Court—like the U.S. Supreme Court
District Courts of Appeal—like the U.S. Courts of Appeal
Circuit Courts—like the U.S. District Courts
