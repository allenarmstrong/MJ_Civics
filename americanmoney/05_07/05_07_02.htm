CreditCredit: an arrangement where a buyer obtains a good or service but pays for it later or over time is an important part of the U.S. economyEconomy: the production and use of goods and services in a society. Used wisely, it can help people own their own homes or start a business. When people misuse credit, they can have many problems. The important thing to remember is credit is not free money.
