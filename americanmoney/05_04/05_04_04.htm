What Do Banks Do?

Banks and other financial businesses in the United States play an important role in our daily lives. They help our society grow. Credit unionsCredit Union: a financial business owned by its members that provides banking services and investment companies also help people and businesses meet their financial goals. Banks, especially the Federal ReserveFederal Reserve: central bank of the United States, also known as the Fed, help ensure the economy runs smoothly.
