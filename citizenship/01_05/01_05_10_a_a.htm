Paths: Answers Text Version

Place each benefit into one of the three categories in which it belongs.

Rights of U.S. Citizens

  • Can serve on a jury
  • Can get a U.S. passport
  • Can vote in national elections
  • Can work for the U.S. government

Native U.S. Citizen Path

  • Born in the United States
  • Born to a U.S. citizen parent
  • Automatic citizenship

Naturalized U.S. Citizen Path

  • Immigrant path to citizenship
  • Must know basic English
  • At least 18 years old
  • Legal, permanent U.S. resident
  • Good moral character
  • Must answer questions about U.S. government and history
  • Must swear the Oath of Allegiance
