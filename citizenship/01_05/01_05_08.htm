What Are the Steps in Naturalization?

Many immigrants wish to become U.S. citizens. Usually, a person must be a legal immigrant to apply. This means the person had permission to enter and remain in the country.

IllegalIllegal: against the law immigrants are people who enter the United States without permission. Americans debate whether to allow some illegal immigrants to stay in certain situations.

Naturalization is for legal immigrants who wish to become U.S. citizens. Let's look more closely at the steps of this process. Maria will guide us through her experience.
