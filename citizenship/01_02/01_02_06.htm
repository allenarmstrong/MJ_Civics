What Do Maps of Cultural Traits Look Like?

We can view maps of cultural regions that relate to a single trait, like language. These traits do not pay attention to state borders. People and ideas move. However, a person from Maine is likely to share more cultural traits with someone from New Hampshire than with someone from New Mexico.

Many nations collect data about its people through a censusCensus: official count of a population, or the process for collecting and recording information about a population. Census data is the base for many maps in this activity.
