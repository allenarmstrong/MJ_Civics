Map Analysis Questions

  1. What type of map is it?
  2. What does the map title tell you about the map?
  3. Who made the map, and when?
  4. What map features are most important to understanding the map?
  5. List specific things you see in this map that you think are important.
  6. What patterns do you see in the map features?
  7. What connections can you make between other maps or what you know and what you see in this map?

Map Interpretation Questions

  1. Why do you think the patterns and connections you noticed in the map exist?
  2. Why do you think this map was created? Explain your response using features you analyzed in the map.
  3. Does the map support or contradict your mental map, or what you already know about this topic or place? Explain your answer.
  4. Write at least one question for the cartographer or mapmaker that this map does not answer.
