Map Tutorial—Locating Places Check Answers

Lesson 3 of 6:

Use your eyes and finger to estimate how many inches are between Los Angeles, California, and the state capital, Sacramento on the map. Then, once you know the distance in inches, multiply that number by 150 to determine the number of miles.

Correct Answer: If you estimated 350 miles, you are correct! Now you should be familiar with absolute location, relative location, and estimating distance.

Lesson 4 of 6:

Self-Check

Question 1 of 11

Select the Texas city which is along the U.S. – Mexico border.

Correct Answer: El Paso

 

Question 2 of 11

Select the state capital which is closest to 40 degrees north, 83 degrees west.

Correct Answer: Columbus

 

Question 3 of 11

Select the U.S. highway which runs from Jacksonville, Florida to Los Angeles, California.

Correct Answer: I-10

 

Question 4 of 11

Select the U.S. landmark which is less than 300 miles from Chicago, Illinois.

Correct Answer: Gateway Arch

 

Question 5 of 11

Select the most southeastern Canadian landmark on this map.

Correct Answer: Montréal Olympic Stadium

 

Question 6 of 11

Select the country which the U.S. borders at about 49 degrees north latitude.

Correct Answer: Canada

 

Question 7 of 11

Select the state capital which is about 500 miles northwest of Des Moines, Iowa.

Correct Answer: Bismark

 

Question 8 of 11

Select the U.S. landmark which borders the states of Arizona and Nevada.

Correct Answer: Hoover Dam

 

Question 9 of 11

Select the capital of Canada.

Correct Answer: Ottowa

 

Question 10 of 11

Select the major line of longitude which is closest to Florida’s Everglades.

Correct Answer: 80 degrees

 

Question 11 of 11

If you drive 750 miles east from Topeka, Kansas, select the state in which you will be (or arrive since you are talking about driving somewhere).

Correct Answer: West Virginia

Correct Answer: Missouri River

Select the U.S. state which has the longest border along the Pacific Ocean.

Correct Answer: California

Question 8 of 10

Question 10 of 10
