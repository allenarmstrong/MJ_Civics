Do You Think These Laws Are Constitutional?

Study each of the situations presented. Determine whether you think the related laws are acceptable under the U.S. ConstitutionU.S. Constitution: plan detailing the structure, functions, and powers of the government of the United States, also known as the Constitution.

Under California law, a person was sent to jail for 50 years up to a life sentence for being caught stealing three times.


The Supreme Court agreed with you.

Case of Lockyer v. Andrade (2003)
The Supreme Court ruled that the California "three strikes" law was constitutional. This law requires the state court to give a much longer jail or prison sentence to those who are convicted of serious crimes three or more times. The Supreme Court ruled that this law is not cruel or unusual punishment, banned by the 8th Amendment.
