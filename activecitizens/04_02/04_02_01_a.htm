Case Practice Text Version

Instructions: Read each situation. Answer "Yes" if you believe the law or laws related to that situation are acceptable under the Constitution. Answer "No" if you believe they are unacceptable.

  1. Under California law, a person was sent to jail for 50 years up to a life sentence for being caught stealing three times.
  2. Natural-born citizens can have their U.S. citizenship taken away as a punishment.
  3. A state government can ban the teaching of any language other than English.
  4. A person who burns the American flag in a public protest can be sentenced to prison.
  5. A police officer may search passengers of a vehicle stopped for a traffic law violation.
