Why Have Rules and Laws?


© 2011 AP Images

We all have rules to follow. Rules exist at home, in schools, in parks, and many places we spend our time. We may not always agree with a rule. However, they exist to protect everyone's safety and well-being and keep society functioning, and most people agree with the need for such rules.

Parents, business owners, teachers, and other adults enforce rules in certain situations. Laws are more official than rules and are enforced by the government. Laws are rules established by the government or another source of authority to regulate people's conduct or activities. Like rules, laws exist to protect safety and order.
