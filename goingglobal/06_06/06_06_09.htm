Which is the best for the United States




The strong and weak dollar can benefit different types of U.S. businesses. U.S. importers are businesses that sell foreign products. They prefer a strong dollar. It makes the prices of imports lower, and so Americans will buy more. U.S. exporters are businesses that sell American products to other countries. They prefer a weak dollar. It makes the prices of exports lower, and so foreign businesses will buy more.
