Recap Quiz: Answers Text Version

  1. A government where one person makes all of the decisions would be an
    c. autocracy–An autocracy is a government where one person holds all the decision-making power. A dictatorship or monarchy is a type of autocracy.
  2. Of the following countries, which trades the most with the United States?
    a. Mexico–Mexico, along with Canada, trades most with the United States. Geography is an important reason, as it is much easier to trade with countries near one's own borders.
  3. Decisions made about war in another country are part of
    d. foreign policy–Foreign policy includes decisions relating to situations in other countries, such as war. Intervention is a possible foreign policy position a country could use.
  4. Which of the following groups is least likely to depend daily on currency exchange?
    a. teachers–Foreign exchange rates have a great effect on the business of importers, exporters, and travelers. Currency exchange affects everyone in the world. However, in this group, teachers are the least likely to notice the effects daily.
  5. The United States, Canada, and Mexico are all
    b. federations–All of these countries in North America are federations. They have strong central governments that share power with state and local governments.
  6. What is a challenge for a country that depends on a single resource or product?
    a. If something happens to its main resource, the country has no other means to trade or support itself.–A country that depends on a single product or resource faces many challenges. It usually must import much more than it exports. If there is a disaster or people stop wanting that product, the country now has nothing to export and no way to buy imports.
  7. Providing education and health care to U.S. citizens is part of
    a. domestic policy–Domestic policy includes programs and decisions within a country, such as education and health care. The U.S. Department of State assists with foreign policy. Diplomacy is also an act of foreign policy.
  8. Which of the following conflicts is an example of how the United States has used almost every foreign policy position?
    d. World War Two–At the beginning of World War Two, the United States remained in isolation. Then, it began using diplomacy and helping its allies with supplies. Finally, it intervened when attacked by Japan in 1941.
  9. Why is using "per capita" data important for comparing countries?
    c. It divides information by population, so you get a more accurate understanding.–"Per capita" means "per person." Countries differ in size and population. Dividing a number, such as GDP, by population tells you about production per person in the country. This is more useful for comparison than the total number.
  10. All of the following are important natural resources in North America, except
    a. diamonds–North America has significant oil, coal, and gold resources. It is not, however, a great producer of diamonds.
  11. Who would be happy if the United States has a strong dollar?
    b. a U.S. importer of Mexican goods–A U.S. importer means a business bringing goods from other countries into the United States. They would prefer a strong dollar because it costs them less to buy foreign goods.
  12. Which of the following international organizations aims to use diplomacy to prevent world war in the future?
    a. United Nations, or UN–The UN was set up after World War Two to bring countries together to solve conflicts. The hope was that diplomacy could prevent world war in the future. NATO is a military alliance. The Red Cross provides disaster relief. The WTO promotes free trade among countries.
