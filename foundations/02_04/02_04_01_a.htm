Situation Quiz Text Version

Answer the following questions. Keep track of which point value you receive.

Question 1

You are embarrassed when neighborhood kids laugh at you for wearing a helmet. In your area, a law states you must wear one when riding a bike. Many teens do not wear helmets. What do you do?

  • Ignore them and ride faster to get away (0 points)
  • Plan a pizza party for teens and have a firefighter talk about bike safety (4 points)
  • Smile as you pass and say, "Smart people protect their brain!" (1 point)

Question 2

You accidently damage the clothes dryer. You feel bad, knowing it will be expensive for your family to replace. Yet you have no money to help. What do you do?

  • Babysit, mow lawns, and complete other small jobs to help earn money for the new dryer. (4 points)
  • Hang washed clothes outside and put them away when dry. (1 point)
  • Apologize to your family and hide in your room. (0 points)

Question 3

You are upset because your best friend borrowed a favorite shirt but has not returned it. Your friend claims to have given it back weeks ago. Now neither of you will speak to each other. You miss your friend and your shirt. What do you do?

  • Wait for your friend to apologize and give the shirt back. (0 points)
  • Save money to replace the shirt and spend time with other friends. (1 point)
  • Ask another friend or a trusted adult to help you and your friend resolve the argument. (4 points)

Add up your total points from all three questions to see how you deal with problmes.

0 points: Are you a problem-avoider? Based on your answers to this quick quiz, it appears you may have a tendency to avoid problems. Keep in mind that facing problems head-on is often the best way to resolve them!

1–3 points: Are you working on your problem-solving skills? Based on your answers to this quick quiz, it appears you may be new to tackling problems on your own. Choosing to respond to a situation is a step in the right direction!

4–9 points: Are you an emerging problem-solver? Based on your answers to this quick quiz, it appears your problem-solving skills are growing and could use some fine-tuning. You chose at least once to tackle a problem head-on and find a solution. Keep finding those opportunities!

12 points: Are you a super problem-solver? Based on your answers to this quick quiz, it appears you are quite confident in your problem-solving skills. You did a great job recognizing how you could solve these dilemmas!
