Definitions and Examples Text Version

Speech

Definition

Communication through spoken words and gestures

Examples

Wearing a shirt with an American flag on it, giving a speech about online classes, telling others why you support a certain person running for political office, making a peace sign with your hand

Press

Definition

All the people involved in gathering and reporting the news

Examples

A newspaper editor commenting on the remarks of political leaders, a news website editor sharing ideas for improving national security, a magazine article writer complaining about health care law

Assembly

Definition

The coming together of people for a common purpose

Examples

A local organization sponsoring a holiday parade, a political leader holding a street rally to gain supporters, a public protest against a new policy, people from a neighborhood meeting to discuss problems with residents allowing pets to roam free

Religious expression

Definition

The communication of religious beliefs, thoughts, or feelings

Examples

People gathering in a religious building, such as a church, to worship together, a group of teens meet to study the Bible and share their thoughts, beginning a meeting with a prayer

Petition

Definition

A formal request, or to make a formal request to government to address a problem

Examples

People signing a letter to show support for government action on a certain issue, sending a personal request for legal help to a member of Congress, talking to a political leader about a problem, gathering signatures from voters as a step toward adding a proposal for a new law to the next election ballot
