What is reliable information? How to perform research

Step 4: Digging Deeper at the Library

Alicia now moves on to the library to seek the librarian’s assistance in her research.

What should Alicia ask the librarian to get the most useful information?

Where can I find your library catalog?

It’s just over there, near the front lobby. I can take you to it if you would like me to.

That’s a great shirt! Where did you get it?

Would you like to find some books about shirts?

Where can I find information on scuba diving?

We actually have an entire section on scuba information that you might want to visit. Would you like me to show you?
