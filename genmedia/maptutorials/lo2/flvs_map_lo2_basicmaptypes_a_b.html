Lesson 8 of 8:

Question 1 of 3:

Select the features you need to make a physical map of the United States.

Answer: Mountain Ranges, Rivers

Question 2 of 3:

Select the features you need to make a political map of the United States.

Answer: Major Cities, State Borders

Question 3 of 3:

Select the features you need to make a thematic map of the United States.

Answer: Interstate Highways, Record Low Temps
