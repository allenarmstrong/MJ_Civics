[Interviewer] What type of service project are you involved in?

[Brianna Frank] I am involved with an organization that builds homes for families throughout my community. Whether affected by bad weather, or hit by the economy, they can really benefit from a house

[Interviewer] How does your service impact your community?

[Brianna Frank] My service impacts my community because I am one little detail in a big picture. When we all work together for a common goal, more houses get to be built at a quicker speed which allows more homes to go to more families.

[Interviewer] What have you learned as a result of your service?

[Brianna Frank] I have learned through my community service that when people push through to a common cause, more things get to be done at a quicker pace and it really helps the family and it really helps get their life settled.

[Interviewer] What motivates you to be an involved member of your community?

[Brianna Frank] I am motivated to participate in this organization because it is a great feeling to give back to your community and to see so many people suffer through these hard times its a great feeling when you see their smile on their face and you know you've done something really great for them.

[Interviewer] What tips would you give to other students who want to make a difference in their own communities?

[Brianna Frank] If you want to do something in your community find something that interests you and find local charities around your community that you can participate in. Usually they have a lot of events going on and its easy to go out and support them.
