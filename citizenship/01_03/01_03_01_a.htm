Trivia Challenge Text Version

  1. My official muffin is the apple muffin. I border Canada and two of the Great Lakes. What state am I?

  2. I have the world's largest prairie dog! I also appear to be in the center of the contiguous United States. What state am I?

  3. Once upon a time, before I was a U.S. state, some of my people declared independence from Mexico. They named me the "Bear Flag Republic." What state am I?

  4. I am a huge state. In fact, people like to say we do everything big here. I share a border with another nation, four states, and a large body of water. What state am I?

  5. One of my parks is known as the crown jewel of the continent! I am part of the West and share a long border with Canada. What state am I?

  6. I was the first official U.S. state, but I am the only state without a national park! What state am I?

  7. Before I was a U.S. state, I was a kingdom with a queen! Ocean water forms my entire border. What state am I?

  8. I am the only state to require a license to teach swimming. People sure swim a lot here. I have many natural springs and water bordering three sides. What state am I?

  9. Rivers border two of my sides. I am the only state whose name begins with two vowels! What state am I?

  10. I am the only state that shares a border with only one other U.S. state. I also happen to border another nation! What state am I?

  11. All my border corners make 90-degree angles. My official bird is the seagull, though I am landlocked. What state am I?

  12. All other U.S. states have "counties," but my people call this state's divisions "parishes." My southern coastline has been a centre of trade for centuries. What state am I?

Select your answers from the following states:

Kansas, Texas, Hawaii, Delaware, Florida, Utah, Maine, Iowa, Louisiana, Montana, California, New York
