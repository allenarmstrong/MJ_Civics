Countries Go To War!

Two countries are at war again over the boundaries of their territories. Both have a historical basis for their claims.

New Talks

New talks between countries in the same region began today. They hope to remove rules that limit trade between them.

Internet Ban Update

The country that just ended its ban on Internet access had a shock today. In just one month, two million of its residents began using the Internet!

More Students

More students are learning multiple foreign languages than ever before. Leaders approve the trend. They say the ability to communicate with people around the world is a necessary skill.
