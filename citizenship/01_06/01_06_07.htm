How Do Good Citizens Support the Common Good at Each Level of Government?

We need government and rules to have peace and order in society. The United States has three main levels of government: local, state, and federalFederal: referring to the central government of the United States, or the form of government in which several states or regions share and divide political powers with a central government. Each level has certain responsibilities. We support government mainly through taxes—for example, income taxesIncome Taxes: money paid to government based on earnings. Everyone benefits from government, so everyone helps pay for it.

Good citizens can support the common good at each level of government. Paying taxes is simply one way to help the government improve society for its citizens. Examine this image to see how paying taxes allows government to provide services at each level.
