The Equal Rights Amendment is one of six proposed amendments to the U.S. Constitution that have not been ratified. Americans debate whether ratification should have a deadline. Let's learn more about the arguments for and against these failed amendment ideas.

Objectives
If you are not taking the course for Advanced credit, you do not need to complete this lesson.

After completing this lesson, you will be able to:

  • identify amendment ideas that have not achieved ratification
  • research and describe arguments for and against an amendment idea
