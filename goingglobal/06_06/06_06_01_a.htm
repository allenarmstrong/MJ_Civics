Fruit Exchange Text Version

  • First image–two teens, one with apples and the other with oranges
    Caption: One teen has apples. The other teen has oranges.
  • Second image– Same teens, now both have some apples and some oranges
    Caption: Each teen wants to have apples and oranges, so they trade some of their fruit.
  • Third image–Same image as the first
    Caption: They meet another day. This time, the teen with apples wants some oranges. However, the teen with oranges does not like the look of the apples.
  • Fourth image–Same teens, the one with apples has both apples and oranges now and is giving money to the teen that only has oranges.
    Caption: They use money to exchange the oranges, since the other teen did not want the apples this time.
