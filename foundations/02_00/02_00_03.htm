Checklist

The Foundations Checklist will help you track your work. You may print the checklist to help you stay organized. It's a good idea to note on the checklist your grade for each assessment to help you stay on track. Reading each page of the lessons and any linked information will help you succeed in this course.


Have you started planning your service project? The sooner you begin work on this project the better. You could work on it with other students to complete your collaboration requirement. The lessons may give you great ideas for service! Don't forget you'll need to complete Part A of your Service Learning project before you complete Segment One of the course. Review the Service Learning Unit for details and then contact your instructor if you have questions.
