Citizenship Test: Answers Text Version

Read the questions presented before you and select the best possible answer.

  1. What is the name of the national anthem?
    1. The Star Spangled Banner
  2. Name one of the two longest rivers in the United States.
    1. Mississippi River
  3. What do we show loyalty to when we say the Pledge of Allegiance?
    1. United States
  4. In what month do we vote for President?
    1. November
  5. Name two national U.S. holidays.
    1. Labor Day and Thanksgiving
  6. Why does the flag have 50 stars?
    1. One star for each state
  7. Name one right only for U.S. citizens.
    1. Freedom to run for federal office
  8. What is one responsibility that is only for United States citizens?
    1. Serve on a jury
  9. What are the two major political parties in the United States?
    1. Democratic and Republican
  10. Why does the flag have 13 stripes?
    1. The stripes represent the original colonies.
