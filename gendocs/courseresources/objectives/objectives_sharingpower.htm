Sharing Power Objectives

After completing this unit, you will be able to:

  • print and use the Sharing Power Checklist
  • complete the Sharing Power Pretest
  • identify federalism
  • differentiate responsibilities of the different levels of government
  • compare the constitutions of the United States and Florida
  • define rule of law and recognize its influence
  • identify sources and types of law
  • illustrate the lawmaking process at different levels of government
    • research a recent bill or law (Advanced)
    • analyze the potential impact of legislation on different levels of government (Advanced)
  • diagram the levels, functions, and powers of courts at the state and federal levels
  • identify concepts and steps of the trial process
  • simulate a jury trial
  • develop a plan to resolve a state or local problem
  • identify government resources to address the issue
  • review the major concepts and terms from the module
  • complete a Discussion-Based Assessment with your instructor
