American Money Objectives

After completing this unit, you will be able to:

  • print and use the American Money Checklist
  • complete the American Money Pretest
  • define scarcity and opportunity cost
  • recognize the relationship between supply, demand, and price
  • recognize economic concepts in the development of the United States
  • explain how profits, incentives, and competition motivate people and groups
  • identify diverse entrepreneurs
  • use geographic terms and tools to explain cultural diffusion
  • explain the principles of market and mixed economies
  • identify U.S. laws and regulations used to promote market competition
  • describe impact of the U.S. banking system
  • explain how economic institutions impact the national economy
  • explain how taxes support the economy at different levels
  • compare the national budget to a personal budget
    • identify the federal spending categories (Advanced)
    • evaluate spending priorities through conducting a survey (Advanced)
  • discuss the role and function of financial institutions in the U.S. economy
  • discuss the relationship between government and financial institutions
  • review the major concepts and terms from the module
  • complete a Discussion-Based Assessment with your instructor
