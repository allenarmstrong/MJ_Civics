What Do I Have to Do?

You should now be ready to interview someone serving your community! In this activity, you will conduct an interview and reflect on the person's service type.

Steps


© 2011 Todd Wamock/Lifesize/Thinkstock
  1. Identify someone to interview. Think about your service project or groups familiar to you. Think of someone who volunteers in at least one of the three ways.
  2. Follow the interview steps. Talk with your parent or guardian about the best way to conduct the interview. With their help, set up a time and place with your interviewee.
  3. Think ahead about the questions you wish to ask. Use the Service Interview Template for basic questions. Include at least one additional question of your own. Take notes during the interview.
  4. Send a thank-you note to your interviewee.
  5. Type your interview notes. Answer the reflection questions from the template.
  6. Save your work into one file. Save your file as "Citizenship 07A your name."

Please view the Service Interview Grading Rubric before starting the assignment.

  1. Complete the reading and activities for this lesson.
  2. Review your CAP file for this lesson.
  3. Complete and submit your Reflection to Citizenship 07A Who Shows Super Citizenship?
