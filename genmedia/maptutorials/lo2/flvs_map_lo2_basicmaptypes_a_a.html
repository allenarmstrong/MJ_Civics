Map Tutorial—Basic Map Types Check Answers Lesson 1

Lesson 1 of 8:

Your friend has three maps. Each one is the same area, but shows different information. Help your friend choose the map she needs for each task.

Question 1 of 3:

Your friend needs some help. Which map will help her determine the current weather in Kansas City, Missouri?

  1. Map 1
  2. Map 2
  3. Map 3


Feedback for selecting "Map 3":  Yes! You correctly chose the weather map, a type of thematic map.

Question 2 of 3:

Now your friend wants to find out what cities are near Kansas City, Missouri. Which map should she use?

  1. Map 1
  2. Map 2
  3. Map 3

Feedback for selecting "Map 1":  Excellent! You correctly chose the map showing cities and borders, a political map.

Question 3 of 3:

Your friend wonders if there are mountains in Kansas. Which map should she use?

  1. Map 1
  2. Map 2
  3. Map 3


Feedback for selecting "Map 2":  Super! You correctly chose the maps showing landforms and bodies of water, a physical map.
