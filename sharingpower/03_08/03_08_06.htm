How Do We Determine the Best Way to Solve the Problem or Issue?

When you find a problem to address, you might think of ways to fix it right away. However, acting without a plan or thinking first about what you will do can result in disaster. Solving the problem will need support from government. It may require a change in public policy.

First you must learn everything you can about the issue. Then you need to think about what would be an acceptable solution. Next you will list features that a public policy must meet for others to consider it a good option. Common criteriaCriterion: a standard used for making a judgment about something to use in judging options include how much it will cost, whether there is wide support, and how easy it will be to start.




In your CAP File, you will follow the steps to create a citizen action plan. Work on this as you continue in the lesson.
