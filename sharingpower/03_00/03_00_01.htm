Why Is Sharing Power Important?

In the United States, ordinary citizensCitizen: a person who is native or naturalized to a country have the power to make a difference in their communities every day. They share this power and responsibility with other people, groups, and government officials.

Yasmin is a middle-school student who volunteers with A Second Chance Puppies & Kittens Rescue (ASCR). This group of volunteers may be working to help animals in your town! Wherever they are, private organizations like ASCR must work with local governments and follow laws as they carry out their goals.
