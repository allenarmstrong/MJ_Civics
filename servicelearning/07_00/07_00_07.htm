What Can You Do?


© 2011 Jupiterimages/Creatas/Thinkstock

Students can serve their communities through a variety of ways such as school volunteer clubs, religious organization activities, and community groups like Boy Scouts or 4-H. Students can choose to provide service on their own.

You might enjoy teaching a younger child to read. You may prefer working with a large group of people to clean up a park. Perhaps you could gather your friends to organize a canned-food drive in your community and deliver the goods to those in need. The possibilities are endless.

Think about your strengths and the groups you belong to in your community. Consider problems in your community. Brainstorm ways you can help make your community better through service.



Make sure to take notes on your ideas throughout this lesson. Thinking now about what you can do in your community will help you complete your service project!
