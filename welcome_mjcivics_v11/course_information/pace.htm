What Is Your Pace?

Choose a pace for completing this course from the guides below. If you wish to change pace during the progression of the course, you must have permission from your instructor, your parent(s) or guardian(s), and your guidance counselor. Think carefully about the time you have each week to devote to this class, and choose your pace wisely.

Print and fill in the appropriate pace guide and place it in your work area for future reference. Please give a copy to your parent(s) or guardian(s).

Pace Option Description
Traditional Complete the course in 36 weeks.
Traditional Advanced Complete the course in 36 weeks.
Accelerated Complete the course in 18 weeks.
Accelerated Advanced Complete the course in 18 weeks.
Second Segment Only Complete the course in 18 weeks.
Second Segment Only Advanced Complete the course in 18 weeks.
Extended Pace
(Requires teacher, parent, and guidance counselor approval.)		 Your individual pace will be determined by your teacher. You will be required to submit a petition for extended pace documenting approval from your parents and your school guidance counselor. When the completed petition form is received, it will be kept on file by your online instructor.
