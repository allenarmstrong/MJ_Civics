Help for Environmental Group Card

Your Name Type or write in your name. Remember that each member of your team must create one of these cards. Therefore, if you have three group members, your team will have three complete cards for three different environmental groups.
Name of Environmental Group and Purpose or Mission Type or write in the group’s name here, such as “Tampa Bay Watch.”
Where You Found the Information Name the person, people, websites, newspapers, magazines, or other sources where you learned about the group. Include how to find this information again, perhaps a phone number or the Web address.
What the Group Does Name the specific actions the group takes, such as how Tampa Bay Watch builds oyster domes and has students in school raise bay grasses. Some groups will have many programs. Be sure to explain what each one does.
How to Contact the Group Who is in charge of the group in your area? What is his or her phone number or email address? If you did not include it above, be sure to note here the group’s website if it has one.
Volunteer Opportunities with this Group Describe how people can help this group. List any programs just for students. Check if it has a calendar and list any upcoming events.
Other Information Use this section to record other useful information you find, such as charts, images, or links to news information on this group.
