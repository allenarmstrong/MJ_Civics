How Does the United States Compare?

The United States is one of the richest countries of the world. However, GDP per capita data does not mean that each person is earning that amount of money per year. It tells us the value of goods and services produced by each person.

People do not share equally in the wealth of a country. That is why a country with a high GDP can also have a high poverty rate.
