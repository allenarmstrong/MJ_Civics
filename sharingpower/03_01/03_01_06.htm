Whom Do You Call?

Remember that the U.S. Constitution states the powers of the national government. The 10th Amendment reserves powers to the states, and each state government has the power to define how local governments operate. Americans have the most contact with their local and state governments and laws because they create most of the laws that affect our daily lives.

Try this activity to learn how the levels of government divide responsibilities to the people.

Make sure you have your CAP File for reference when you begin this activity. Make notes in your CAP file as you complete the activity.
