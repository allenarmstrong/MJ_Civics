Map Tutorial

Some of the remainder lessons in this unit will use a map interactive. These interactives will function the same way. The following icons are used within the interactive to allow you to navigate among the map. Not all maps will use all of the controls.

Info

Once you start the map intearctive you can always refer to the information button located at the top left of the page. The information button will list the instructions for the current map interactive and is represented by the red icon.

Compass Rose

You can navigate the map interactive by using the compass rose. You can move north, south, east, and west. The compass rose is represented by the blue circle with four arrows and north labeled with an "N".

Note: You can also navigate by clicking your mouse while over the map and dragging it.

Slider Bar

When you want to view the map closer, you can zoom in using the slider. You can also zoom out using the slider bar. To zoom in drag the slider to the plus sign or to the right. To zoom out drag the slider to the negative sign or to the left. The slider is represented by the blue bar.

Selector

The selector will show you any items that you have to work with. The selector is represented by a blue circle.

Selector Item

The selector item is the actual item that you drag to the appropriate place on the map. The selector item is represented by a shape or word.

Previous

The previous button allows you to navigate to the previous portion of the interactive. The previous button is represented by the blue circle with an arrow pointing to the left and labeled "Prev".

Next

The next button allows you to navigate to the next portion of the interactive. The next button is represented by the blue circle with an arrow pointing to the right and labeled "Next".

Submit

The submit button allows you to submit your answer to a question. The submit button is represented by a blue circle with a check mark.
