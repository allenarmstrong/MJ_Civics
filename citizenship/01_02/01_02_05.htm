What Are Cultural Regions?

A cultural region is a geographic area with shared cultural traits. People of many different cultures can live in one area. Yet the majority of people in any one area tend to be part of one cultural group.

North America includes many nations and regions. Mapmakers and governments divide nations into regions. People within these regions usually share many cultural traits.

Remember, though, that culture is transnational. We can draw lines on a map, but people will still move and share ideas!
