The government of the United States was founded by concerned citizens, working together to solve problems. In its early days, the United States faced many challenges. Leaders worked together to design a government that would protect the rights and meet the needs of its people. In this unit, you will learn about the foundations of the U.S. government

Keep track of your learning using the CAP file for each lesson. Print your CAP files to take notes. Be sure to place them together in a safe location. Taking notes will help you prepare for assessments, including the Discussion-Based Assessment and exams.
