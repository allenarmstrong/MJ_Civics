Review: Answers Text Version

  1. The United States is a permanent member of the Security Council in which international organization? United Nations (UN)
  2. Which international organization has people all over the world helping bring emergency relief to victims of war and natural disaster? Red Cross
  3. The United States is part of a military alliance that responded to the September 11, 2001 attacks. What alliance is it? North Atlantic Treaty Organization (NATO)
  4. What country has the United States helped defend from communism since shortly after World War II ended? South Korea
  5. What country did the United States hold as territory after a war with Spain? Cuba
  6. Thousands of Americans serve people in other countries each year to improve their lives and good will toward the United States. What organization do they join? Peace Corps
  7. In what conflict did the United States first stay isolated, then helped with supplies, and then eventually declared war itself? World War II
  8. What conflict do U.S. and other world leaders regret they did not act to stop? Rwanda 1994 genocide
  9. If the leaders of two countries would like help settling a dispute, where can they go for help? International Court of Justice
