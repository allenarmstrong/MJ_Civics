What Are Interest Groups?

One way that active citizens can choose to influence government is through an interest groupInterest group: a group of people with common goals who work together to promote their cause. An interest group is a group of people with common goals who work together to promote their cause. A primary goal of many interest groups is to influence elected officials to make a change in public policyPublic policy: a course of action accepted or used by a government to respond to a problem that meets its goals.

For example, people who believe the government needs to do more to reduce pollution might form an environmental interest group or join an existing one. The interest group would likely meet and plan strategies to contact as many leaders as possible about the problem. They would express what they agree should happen to fix it.
