What Do I Have to Do?


© 2011 AP Images

You should now be an expert on resources and products! Now you will use your learning to prepare a country profile.

Imagine you work for the U.S. Central Intelligence Agency, or CIA. Your job is to analyze "intelligence," or information, about other countries. The director has Intelligence from several countries. You are to study the intelligence for one country from the list. The director included information on the United States for comparison. You will write a report to the director that answers these questions:

  • What natural resources does the country have that are available in North America?
  • What natural resources does the country have that are not available in North America?
  • What natural resources might the country need that are in North America?
  • What are its major industries? What are the risks to these industries?
  • Is the country's economy more or less diverse than the United States? Explain your answer.
  • What opportunities does the country have to increase its diversification? Explain your answer.

Use the maps and activities in the lesson to help you answer the questions.

Please view the Report Grading Rubric before starting the assignment.

  1. Complete the reading and activities for this lesson.
  2. Review your CAP file for this lesson.
  3. Complete and submit your CIA Report to Going Global 03 Why Should Nations Diversify?
