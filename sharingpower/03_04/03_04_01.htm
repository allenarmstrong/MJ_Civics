Where Do We Get Ideas for Laws and Policies?

Examine this photograph. Is there a lawLaw: a rule established by government or other source of authority to regulate people's conduct or activities that should have prevented this problem? Often, people have ideas for new rules, laws, or policies because something went wrong. They hope to prevent the same situation in the future. Of course, if a law is already in place, then people must follow it to ensure the problem does not happen again!

Maybe no visible problem exists, but people still have ideas for ways to improve areas of life. This is the purpose of public policyPublic Policy: a course of action accepted or used by a government to respond to a problem . Public policy includes all the ways government responds to a public issue.
