True or False: Answers Text Version

Determine whether the statements are true or false.

  1. The U.S. government exists because of citizen consent.
    True
  2. U.S. citizens may break laws if they do not agree with them.
    False
  3. People in the United States pay taxes to three main levels of government.
    True
  4. Adult U.S. citizens must serve in the military or on juries when required by law.
    True
  5. Minors cannot volunteer in local communities.
    False
  6. Voting is a duty required by law.
    False
  7. The government provides protection and services to citizens.
    True
  8. Speaking up for someone else's rights is showing great citizenship.
    True
