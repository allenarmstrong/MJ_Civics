How Can We Preserve Culture?

A stewardSteward: a person put in charge of taking care of something, like property protects property. Stewards of culture are careful around cultural landmarks. Weather and handling can damage structures and artifactsArtifact: an object made by humans. Protecting symbols of culture ensures their enjoyment for posterityPosterity: future generations of society.

Good citizens know that being a steward is part of their civic responsibility. They encourage others to respect cultural symbols and landmarks. Can you tell which images show a steward in action?
