What is Jurisdiction? Answers Text Version

Instructions: Read each situation. Choose "state" or "federal" to determine which court system will have original jurisdiction!

Scenario 1

Police arrest Eliza Suspect. They accuse her of losing control of her car and smashing it into the side of a police station. In which court level, state or federal, will this case begin?

  • State

    Feedback—“Yes! Juries usually hear cases and determine verdicts in U.S. District Courts.”
  • Federal

    Feedback—“The correct answer is State. State courts have original jurisdiction over accidents.”

Scenario 2

Ingrid Inventor, who lives in Idaho, owns a U.S. patent by federal law for a soda that protects teeth. She is suing Sal Sudsy for allegedly copying her recipe and selling it as his own product in South Dakota. In which court level, state or federal, will this case begin?

  • State

    Feedback—“The correct answer is Federal. Federal courts have original jurisdiction over issues of federal, or U.S. law.”
  • Federal

    Feedback—“Yes! Federal courts have original jurisdiction over issues of federal, or U.S. law.”
