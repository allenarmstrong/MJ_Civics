Step 3 – Discuss and Determine the Best Response

Brainstorm all possible responses (make more rows if necessary). What could be the outcome for this response? Would the outcome help or hurt the conflict? Would the outcome help or hurt relations between the United States and the rest of the world?
































































































As a team, you should agree on how the United States should respond. This should not simply be picking one team member’s idea, but rather an agreement between all members on the best action. Your team should have details on the response in the chart above. Be sure to note which reasons convinced all the members to support that response.
