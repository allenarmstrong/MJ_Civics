What Are the Types of Service?

Super citizens serve their communities in a variety of ways. We classify service into three types. The types of service are direct, indirect, and advocacyAdvocacy: active spoken or written support for a cause or position. You saw an example of each in the introduction slide.



Brainstorm how people working through each of these types can come together for a common cause. With other students, create a plan that involves each type of service.
