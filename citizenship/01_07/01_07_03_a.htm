Service Text Version

Direct: Direct service is where you interact directly with the person or people you are helping. Teens can visit the reseidents of a nursing home. This teen meets the people she helps face to face.

Indirect: Indirect service is where you help people without working directly with them. These teens are collecting toys for needy children. They will not meet the children who receive the toys, but the action still benefits the needy children.

Advocacy: Advocacy is using your right to express opinions through speech and writing to help others. He hopes to make a change through his ideas that will benefit the city's youth.
