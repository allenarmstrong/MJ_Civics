What is Globalization?

  • Tea–made in Sri Lanka.
  • Kettle–made in China
  • Jam–made in France
  • Jeans–made in China
  • Shoes–made in Vietnam

Globalization refers to the way individual nations of the world are becoming more connected to each other. And in the process, independent. As the cost of international travel and communication falls, and governments around the world move to open market economies, things that happen in one country, increasingly effects what happens in other countries.
