What Do I Have to Do?

Now, you are ready for your Citizenship Unit Exam! Before entering the exam, be sure you have:

  • a distraction-free environment
  • plenty of time to complete the exam
  • completed all required assignments
  • reviewed all of your notes, activities, and reading from this module
  • replayed any video, audio, and activities in this module if needed

Complete and submit your Exam to Citizenship 09 Unit Exam.

If you have completed all units within Segment One, please continue to the Segment One Exam Review. If you are taking the course for advanced credit, be sure to take only the Advanced Segment One Exam.
