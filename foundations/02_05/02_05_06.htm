Who Works in the Branches of the Government?

You saw the basic structure of each branch. Each branch functions according to law and the idea of separation of powersSeparation of Powers: dividing government powers into legislative, executive, and judicial branches. The branches are set up differently and have distinct responsibilities. Each branch has specific powers and limits, yet they have an equal amount of power in government, helping to keep it balanced.
