What is Jurisdiction? Text Version

Instructions: Read each situation. Choose "state" or "federal" to determine which court system will have original jurisdiction!

Scenario 1

Police arrest Eliza Suspect. They accuse her of losing control of her car and smashing it into the side of a police station. In which court level, state or federal, will this case begin?

  • State
  • Federal

Scenario 2

Ingrid Inventor, who lives in Idaho, owns a U.S. patent by federal law for a soda that protects teeth. She is suing Sal Sudsy for allegedly copying her recipe and selling it as his own product in South Dakota. In which court level, state or federal, will this case begin?

  • State
  • Federal

Scenario 3

Mickey Maritime is a sailor rescued by the U.S. Coast Guard after a pirate attack. U.S. officials tracked and arrested the pirates, who were not U.S. citizens. Mickey will testify against the pirates at trial. In which court level, state or federal, will this case begin?

  • State
  • Federal

Scenario 4

Spencer Seltzer from South Carolina is facing trial for robbing a fast-food restaurant. In which court level, state or federal, will this case begin?

  • State
  • Federal

Scenario 5

Claudia Careless wrecked Scoot Moped's $900 electric scooter. Scoot is suing Claudia to recover the cost of the scooter. In which court level, state or federal, will this case begin?

  • State
  • Federal

Scenario 6

Ronnie Rights was arrested for printing counterfeit, or fake, money. In which court level, state or federal, will this case begin?

  • State
  • Federal
