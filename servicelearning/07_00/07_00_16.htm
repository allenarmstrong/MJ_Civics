What Do I Have to Do?

You will submit information about your service experience to your instructor in two parts. Be sure to start on Part A as soon as possible. Part A must be submitted before you complete Segment One of the course. Part B must be submitted before you complete Segment Two of the course. If you are only taking one segment, then you must complete both Part A and Part B in the same segment.

Part A: Investigate and Plan!

  1. Complete the Investigate Sheet.
  2. Complete the Plan Sheet.
  3. Post a description of your issue and your service project plan in the discussion area.
  4. Respond to at least two classmates' post in the discussion area. Share information that may help your classmates. See if you can collaborate with others to complete your service project. Share ideas of how service relates to your course lessons. Review the Discussion Guidelines before posting to the discussion area.
  5. Combine your work into one file using copy and paste. Include a copy of your original discussion post, your two responses, the Investigate Sheet, and the Plan Sheet.
  6. Submit this File to Service Part A.

Part B: Reflect and Celebrate!

  1. Complete the Reflect Sheet.
  2. Post a paragraph summary of your reflection in the discussion area.
  3. Respond to at least one classmate's post in the discussion area. Share your own ideas about service improvement. Celebrate your classmates' experiences! Review the Discussion Guidelines before posting to the discussion area.
  4. Create a presentation about your service experience. You may choose your format. Some ideas include a podcast, song, slide show, or blog. It should include at least one paragraph about how your service was meaningful and valuable to the community and at least one photograph of you in action.
  5. Combine your work into as few files as possible (one if possible!) using copy and paste. Include a copy of your original discussion post, your two responses, the Reflect Sheet, your presentation, and your photograph.
  6. Submit your Product to Service Part B.
