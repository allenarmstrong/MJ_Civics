Was the Declaration of Independence Easy to Prepare?

Delegates to the Continental Congress knew they were taking a great risk. Talking about independence was a serious matter. This was treasonTreason: a betrayal or act of disloyalty toward one's own country against Britain and punishable by death. Yet the king ignored colonists' attempts to seek redressRedress: to make up or make amends for a loss or wrongful action in the previous years.

British soldiers were arriving ready for war while they debated independence. Watch this video to learn more about the ideas behind and challenges of writing the Declaration of Independence.
