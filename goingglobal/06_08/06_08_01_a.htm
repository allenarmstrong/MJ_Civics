Government Roundup!
The world has many different types of government. The type affects who holds decision-making power in the country. The United States is a representative democracy.

World Geo-Hunt!
Geography has a great influence on the development of a country. The resources and challenges a country faces can affect its standard of living. We can use gross domestic product (GDP) to measure standard of living.

Diversify!
A country's natural resources help shape the goods and services it produces. Countries trade products they can make well for others they want or need.

Foreign or Domestic?
Currency is important to trade. Countries need to have a way to exchange their currencies. Supply and demand determine the values of currencies.

Got Cash?
Building ties with others around the world promotes good will and peace. The U.S. government and people are members of many international organizations.
