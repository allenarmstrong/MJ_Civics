What Is Economics?


Economics is the study of how people get the goods and services they need and want. We all have wants and needs. Perhaps you at one time said, "I need it," and an adult responded with, "No, you only want it." Needs are things like food, shelter, and clothing. Wants, however, are unlimited. In your CAP File, make a list of things you want to have, places you want to visit, and experiences you want to try. Does the list ever truly end?

CommunitiesCommunity: a group of people who live in the same area, have a common background, or share similar interests have wants and needs too. Your city might need to repair roads that are breaking up. Leaders might want to build a new bridge to encourage new construction in a different area. The list of wants in a community is endless as well, as people within it can have many ideas for improvement.

You can work together with your fellow online students to discuss a community group's "wants" and its options. Select the Collaboration Option for details on using this activity to meet your collaboration requirement.
