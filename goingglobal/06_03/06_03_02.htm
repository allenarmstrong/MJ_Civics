The Candy Explosion store probably has more customers because it can appeal to more people with its variety. Maybe the Gumballs Galore store attracts some customers because it offers special gumballs not available anywhere else. A country's economyEconomy: the production and use of goods and services in a society can be like one of these stores. Depending on its resourcesResource: a material or item that can be used to produce something new, it may produce a variety of goods and services for trade. If it has fewer resources, it may only be able to produce a limited range of goods and services. Let's learn more about how resources affect a country's economy.

Objectives

After completing this lesson, you will be able to:

  • compare and contrast a single resource economy with a diversified economy
  • use maps to describe characteristics of natural resources in North America
