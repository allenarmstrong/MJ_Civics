Yasmin is a great citizen who is using her time and talents to make a difference. A Second Chance Puppies & Kittens Rescue, like similar groups, must follow the laws and can encourage officials to make better ones to help animals.

In the United States, the federal government, state governments, and local governments all have a say in how the people are governed. The people themselves have power as well. Public PolicyPublic Policy: a course of action accepted or used by a government to respond to a problem and the court system are important means through which citizens can contribute to society and seek justiceJustice: fairness under the law. In this unit, you will learn how governments share power, why and how laws are made, and how the courts help citizens seek justice.

Keep track of your learning using the CAP file for each lesson. Print your CAP files to take notes. Be sure to place them together in a safe location. Taking notes will help you prepare for assessments, including the Discussion-Based Assessment and exams.
