What Happens Now?


© 2011 AP Images

Car accidents happen every day in the United States. You may have passed a few or been in an accident yourself. Did you ever consider all the consequences of a wreck? Doctors may need to treat injuries. Mechanics may fix vehicles. Road workers may clean up debris and fix any damage to structures like streetlights. What do the police do?

The police examine the accident scene to make a report of the accident. They determine the causes of the accident and list the damages. Copies of the report go to several people, including the drivers involved in the accident. The story does not end there, though. Sometimes, when the injuries are healed and the vehicles are fixed, the drivers still have legalLegal: relating to the law, or permitted by law issues to deal with. The police report may become important in the court system.
