Work Together Text Version

Scenario: Two kids are competing from their lemonade stand.

A girl is setting up a lemonade stand at the next house down from a boy's lemonade stand. The boy looks surprised.

The girl sets a lower price than the boy, more customers at her stand.

The boy puts out sign "Only fresh, organic lemons used here!" and now more customers are at his stand.

The girl walks over to boy and says, "Maybe we should work together. I bet we could make and sell more lemonade together than we can apart." The boy looks like he is considering the offer.
