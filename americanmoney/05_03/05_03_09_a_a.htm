Match the Statement

  • Supported a free market economy with little or no government regulation Adam Smith
  • Thought a market economy was best with government protection from economic problems John Maynard Keynes
  • Believed market economies were unfair and government should make economic decisions Karl Marx
  • Prevents a business from driving out the competition and making unfair profits Antitrust Law
  • Protects an inventor's creation so others can not steal and profit from the idea Patent Law
  • Are what to produce, how to produce it, and for whom will it be produced Three economic questions

Match the Cause and Effect

  • Companies will have to obtain permission and give payment to the inventor to produce and sell the invention. An inventor obtains a patent for a new invention.
  • The company that has a monopoly must split into separate companies or send customers to a different company. A federal court rules that a certain company is violating antitrust law.
  • Businesses that sell the item change their prices to reflect the new regulation. Some have to increase their prices and lose customers. Those who lower their prices might gain customers. A regulation sets a minimum price for a certain product.
