How Far Can 20 Dollars Go?

Imagine you have 20 dollars. You go to the store to spend your cash. How will you choose to spend it? Try this activity to see a few options.

Drag what you want to buy into the cart
and be sure to hit Check Out.
Book = $8
Candy = $3
CD = $15
Gum = $1
Movie = $10
Magazine = $5
Shirt = $12
Shoes = $18
Soda = $2
Video Game = $20

Cash Spent: $0.00
Cash Left: $20.00

©2011 Photodisc/Brand X Pictures/Comstock/PhotoObjects.net/Stockbyte/Thinkstock

At checkout you were required to pay a 5% sales tax. Did you think about this before you checked out? Did you have enough money to buy the items and pay for the tax? If not try again keeping the 5% sales tax in mind.
