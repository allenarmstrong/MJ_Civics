What Rights Do Crime Suspects Have?

Al Fraydo is 18 and in his first year of college. He wants to be a chef and has a job at a local restaurant. For fun he enjoys hanging out with his buddies and trying new restaurants. Watch what happens to Al one afternoon.

Please click Begin to start the slide show.

Officer: Are you Al Fraydo?

Al: Yes. Can I help you, officer?

Officer: You are under arrest.

Al: Why am I being arrested? What's going on?

Officer: You know. Stop pretending.

Al: Where are we going?

Officer: I'm taking you to the county jail, of course.

Al: What about an attorney? Can I contact anyone and let them know what's happening?

Officer: Attorney, ha. You messed up big time, buddy.

Al: But what did I do? I haven't broken any laws!

Officer: Listen, buddy. Acting like you don't know can hurt you later. It would be better to admit it.
