You should now be ready take a closer look at the tie between citizenship and an ecological issue in your area! In this activity, you will focus on your community. You viewed maps of ecological issues earlier in the lesson. Choose an issue from those maps that affects your local community. You will include a map of your local area in the assessment like the one in your CAP file.






You have several options. Select an option to view detailed instructions.

Option A: Make a Picture    Option B: Write a Letter    Option C: Read an Article    Option D: Speak to a Leader

You only need to complete one option. Be sure to check your spelling and grammar. Save your file as "Citizenship 04 your name."



Have you begun planning your service project? This assignment could help you complete it. Take some time to review the Service Learning unit now.



Please view the Ecology Issue Grading Rubric before starting the assignment. If you choose to complete your collaboration in this lesson, select the Collaboration Option for details on meeting your collaboration requirement.

  1. Complete the reading and activities for this lesson.
  2. Review your CAP file for this lesson.
  3. Complete and submit your Ecology Issue to Citizenship 04 How Are Citizenship and Ecology Connected?
