Collaboration Assessment Guide Rubric

  Excellent Good Needs Improvement Score Yourself!
Task Description, Peer- and Self-evaluation 20 points

  • detailed task description
  • ratings for team and self
  • thoughtful comments on performance of team and self
  • reviews consistent with evidence and project
 15 points

  • detailed task description
  • ratings for team and self
  • somewhat thoughtful comments on performance of team and self
  • reviews consistent with evidence and project
 10 points

  • task description
  • ratings for team and self
  • comments on performance of team and self
  
Reflection 20 points

  • detailed contributions of self and group
  • great insight into collaborative efforts
  • specific areas for improvement
 15 points

  • detailed contributions of self and group
  • some insight into collaborative efforts
  • specific areas for improvement
 10 points

  • contributions of self and group
  • limited insight into collaborative efforts
  
Evidence 10 points

  • at least two pieces of evidence to demonstrate successful collaboration
  • clear, easy-to-identify evidence
 8 points

  • at least two pieces of evidence to demonstrate successful collaboration
  • somewhat clear and easy-to-identify evidence
 5 points

  • at least one piece of evidence to demonstrate successful collaboration
  • somewhat clear and easy-to-identify evidence
