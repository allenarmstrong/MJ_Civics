Lesson 2 of 8:

Screen 1 of 4:

Image of Earth in two-dimensional representation with a vertical line and horizontal line dividing the map into four equal parts. A circle with the letter "A" is placed in the upper-left section over North America, the letter "B" is placed in the upper-right section over Asia, the letter "C" is placed in the lower-left section over South America, and the letter "D" is placed in the lower-right section over Oceania.

Which coordinate point would be labeled in south latitude and west longitude?

Correct Answer: C. Lower-left section over South America

Lesson 8 of 8:

Self-Check Activity

Question 1 of 7: Click on the North Pole.

Correct Answer: Student should choose the area at the very top of the map.

Question 2 of 7: Click on the North 30 degree line of Latitude.

Correct Answer: Student should choose the third horizontal grid line above the equator.

Question 3 of 7: Click on the Equator.

Correct Answer: Student should choose the horizontal line that runs through the center of the map.

Question 4 of 7: Click on the Prime Meridian.

Correct Answer: Student should choose the vertical line that runs through the center of the map.

Question 5 of 7: Click on the Eastern Hemisphere.

Correct Answer: Student should choose either section on the right side of the map.

Question 6 of 7: Click the edge of the map that points west.

Correct Answer: Student should choose the area at the left-most point of the map.

Question 7 of 7: Click on the East 60 degree line of Longitude.

Correct Answer: Student should choose the fifth vertical line to the right of the Prime Meridian.
