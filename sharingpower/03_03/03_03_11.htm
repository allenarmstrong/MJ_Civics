What Do I Have to Do?

You should now be a rule of law master! Now you will apply your learning to realistic situations.

Steps

  1. Read each case in this Assessment Template. You may use the template to complete and submit your work.
  2. Determine the source and type of law that relates to each case.
  3. Determine the purpose of the law related to each case. Is the law intended to protect people's safety or people's rights? Explain your response and thoughts on what could happen if the law did not exist.
  4. Do you think the person or group have a valid case? In other words, should government change the law or make an exception? Explain the reasons behind your response.
  5. Save your work in one file. Name your file "Sharing Power 03 your name."

Please view the Rule of Law Analysis Grading Rubric before starting the assignment. If you choose to complete your collaboration in this lesson, select the Collaboration Option for details on meeting your collaboration requirement.
