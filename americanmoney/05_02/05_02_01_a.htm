Interview Text Version

[Interviewer] What is the name of your business?

[Nick Doherty] The name of my business is nicholasdoherty.com.

[Interviewer] What good or service does your business offer?

[Nick Doherty] I write apps for other companies as well as for myself, it's published on the Apple Apps store.

[Interviewer] How did you get the idea for your business?

[Nick Doherty] So I got an iTouch for my birthday a few years ago. And when I was looking at the apps, I realized that they all were horrible. And I knew I could make them a lot better .. um so... and I wanted too. And I thought that would be a really fun project. So, I went to Stanford for three weeks to take courses on programming. Then when I came back I did a little more studying and started practicing. It just progressed until it got to where it is now.

[Interviewer] Where do you operate your business?

[Nick Doherty] I operate my business in my house.

[Interviewer] How do you find new customers?

[Nick Doherty] I have a website, so if people are searching for app developers or maybe they'll get linked to it. I have a website with all the information about how it works and stuff. And I have a contact form on there or they can easily contact me if they have information or would like to request my service.

[Interviewer] Who helps you to run your business?

[Nick Doherty] So, whenever I have questions with the business side, I always turn to my mom, because she's always really good with the business side of things. And she has a lot of experience with it.

[Interviewer] What have you learned from starting and running a business?

[Nick Doherty] [Chuckle] I've learned that it's not easy. It definitely takes a lot of time. And you need a really good idea to get started. And you need to have a want to continue that and have a passion for what you do.

[Interviewer] What motivated you to start the business?

[Nick Doherty] So, besides the fact that I wanted to improve the apps in the App Store from when I got the iTouch for my birthday. I also wanted a lot of spending money in my pocket. And I knew that doing apps would be a very good way to do that.

[Interviewer] Has that been successful for you?

[Nick Doherty] It has been very successful for me.

[Interviewer] What advice would you give another teen looking to start a business?

[Nick Doherty] I would say you just have to have one really good idea. Something that you love to do. And then find a way to make money from that. Then just devote a lot of time to it. And keep working at it, because it doesn't come easy. It comes with time.
