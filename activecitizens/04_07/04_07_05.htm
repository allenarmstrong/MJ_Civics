What do I have to do?

You should now be an expert on elections! Now you will explain what you learned through your experience in Concord City.

Imagine that when you finished voting, a reporter from ConcordTV requested that you answer a few questions. You politely agree. Answer each of the Election Reflection Questions in your own words. Each response should be a complete paragraph of at least three to five sentences. Be sure to check your work for spelling and grammar.



Please view the Election Reflection Rubric before starting the assignment. If you choose to complete your collaboration in this lesson, select the Collaboration Option for details on meeting your collaboration requirement.

  1. Complete the reading and activities for this lesson.
  2. Review your CAP files for all lessons in the unit.
  3. Complete and submit your Election Reflection to Active Citizens 07 How Do Elections Impact Society?.
