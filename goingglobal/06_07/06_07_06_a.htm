Collaboration Option

This lesson contains a collaboration activity. You will need to complete at least one of the collaboration activities found in lessons Active Citizens 08, American Money 01, American Money 05, Going Global 05, and Going Global 07. Only one collaboration activity is required for each segment of this course. Review the "Getting Started" section of the course for other helpful information about meeting this requirement.

Use the Cabinet Brainstorm Chart to help organize your information. After you complete the collaboration activity, you will submit the Collaboration Assessment Guide and Collaboration Project. Explore the details of this project to decide if it is the right one for you! If you choose this option, your submission will be evaluated based on the Collaboration Assessment Guide Rubric and Collaboration Project Rubric.
