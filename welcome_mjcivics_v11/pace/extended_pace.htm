Extended Pace Chart

Under certain circumstances, students may work at an extended pace. The decision to work at an extended pace must be made by the instructor, parent or guardian, and a school guidance counselor where applicable. If you feel you need to be on the extended pace, please contact your instructor to discuss your situation and determine your eligibility for working at a slower pace.

Once the decision is made that you will work at an extended pace, you must fill out the petition form to work at an extended pace and have it signed by the appropriate people. You will be required to send a copy of the signed form to your instructor.
