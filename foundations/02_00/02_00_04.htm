It is time to take your Foundations Pretest. Remember that this pretest does not count as part of your grade. It measures your current knowledge of material in the course. This will help your instructor identify areas where you may need more support.

Please work without notes or help from others. It's okay if you do not understand the concepts. You will benefit from completing the lessons and assessments that appear later in the unit.
