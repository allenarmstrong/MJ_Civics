Self-Check Part One Text Version

Part One

  1. People who want to start a business can use their savings and ask a bank for a loan to get started. True
  2. Your saved money is safer at home than in a bank account. False
  3. Banks only pay interest on deposited money to people who do not have loans. False
  4. Banks use deposited money to make loans to other people and businesses. True
  5. If you get a loan, you will pay back more money than you borrowed because of interest fees. True
  6. A bank is a business that seeks to make profits. True
  7. People who buy goods and services instead of saving their money hurt the economy. False
  8. A person who wants a loan should shop around for the lowest possible interest rate. True
  9. The Federal Reserve oversees and regulates banking activities in the United States. True
