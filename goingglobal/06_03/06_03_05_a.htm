Scenarios Text Version

Chocolate Land and Lollipop Land are two island countries in a warm climate. Both grow large amounts of sugarcane.

Lollipop Land's main crop is sugar. The people there focus on making lollipops from it. The country is famous around the world for its lollipops. Most people work in the sugar cane fields or in the factories that make the lollipops. The people have been using the same recipes for over 100 years. They are very proud of their traditions and resist new ideas that would change their ways.

Chocolate Land grows cocoa beans and many crops in addition to sugar. It also has dairy farms. Its major products are cocoa powder, chocolate candies and desserts, milk, butter, cheese, and beef. The people grow some fruits and grains, mostly used to make interesting chocolate flavors. They also make lollipops, including chocolate ones, but not as many. Chocolate Land also has a theme park that attracts tourists. There you can ride chocolate-themed rides and learn the history of making chocolate on the island. Hotels are near the park as well as shops selling shirts and postcards.

Scenario 1

Tropical storms hit both islands. The storms destroy the sugar cane fields of both countries.

Which country do you think will have a more difficult recovery?

  1. Chocolate Land
  2. Lollipop Land
  3. Neither, they will have an equally difficult recovery.

Scenario 2

Technology Land, a place with little farming but plenty of Internet companies, is studying both Lollipop Land and Chocolate Land. The leaders of Technology Land are interested in trading with a country that can supply a few of their food needs. They hope that, in return, they could export some of their technology products to the new trade partner.

Which country do you think will be more interesting to the leaders of Technology Land?

  1. Chocolate Land
  2. Lollipop Land
  3. They are equally interesting.

Scenario 3

An inventor discovers how to convert sugar into energy to fuel machines. It can replace oil and gasoline. Both Chocolate Land and Lollipop Land import oil and gasoline from other countries.

Which country is more likely to use this discovery for its own benefit?

  1. Chocolate Land
  2. Lollipop Land
  3. Neither is likely to use the discovery.

Scenario 4

A worldwide news site reports that sugar is the number one cause of disease in humans. Doctors around the globe are urging patients to stop eating sugar and foods that contain it.

Which country will suffer more because of this news?

  1. Chocolate Land
  2. Lollipop Land
  3. They will suffer equally.

Scenario 5

Unfortunately, Technology Land finds itself at war. Several countries are in conflict over control of land. They are focusing on their war and all trade has stopped. The countries at war were big trading partners for Chocolate Land and Lollipop Land.

Which country will have a more difficult time providing for its people as long as the war continues?

  1. Chocolate Land
  2. Lollipop Land
  3. Neither will have a more difficult time than the other.
