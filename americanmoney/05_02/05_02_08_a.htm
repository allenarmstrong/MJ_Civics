Timeline Text Version

(Image: Television showing Ronald McDonald)
1965–Ronald McDonald, the clown mascot of McDonald's®, appears in his first television commercial. Households across the United States see the ad.

(Image: Map of North America)
1967–The first McDonald's® locations open in Canada and Puerto Rico. Today they are in 117 countries around the world.

(Image: Drive thru window at McDonalds)
1975–The first McDonald's drive-thru opens near an army base. Soldiers were not allowed to get out of their vehicles wearing their uniforms. McDonald's® creates the drive-thru as a service to the soldiers.

(Image: Map of Japan)
1978–A McDonald's® opens in Kanagawa, Japan. This is the 5,000th McDonald's® to open.

(Image: McDonald's nutrition poster)
2006–McDonald's® makes nutrition information available to customers. American culture demands not only fast food, but also the information to make healthier choices.
