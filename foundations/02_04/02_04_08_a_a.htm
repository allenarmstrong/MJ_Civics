Sentence Order: Answers Text Version

The following sentences are placed in the correct order.

The Articles of Confederation created the first central government of the United States.

It lasted 10 years and created laws for new territory.

However, it was too weak to address some important issues, such as federalism, separation of powers, and individual rights.

As a result, the Founding Fathers decided to create a new constitution.

This new constitution's Preamble explains the purpose for the document and government.

The Constitution of the United States is based on seven principles that helped address weaknesses from the Articles of Confederation.

It addressed federalism by describing the relationship between the states and federal government. It also separates the federal government into three branches. To protect individual rights, a Bill of Rights was added to the document.
