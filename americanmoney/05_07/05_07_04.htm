What Are the Advantages and Disadvantages of Credit?

Credit has the potential to help or ruin a budgetBudget: plan or record of income and expenses, depending on how people use it. It has many benefits for people and the overall economy, while also presenting challenges if people are not careful with it.

Credit helps the economy grow. Imagine you want to start your own surf shop. Credit can help you obtain the store location, decorate it, and fill it with goods to sell. You might use a computer system to track your business's sales and spending. You pay back the credit as your business grows. Without credit, opening the surf shop would have been too costly for one person. Your business gives back by sponsoring a surf league in the community.

Credit is a benefit here in your local economy. Your use of credit benefits many groups, such as the bank and the supplier of your surfboards. The buying and selling of goods and services helps create jobs and helps businesses succeed.
