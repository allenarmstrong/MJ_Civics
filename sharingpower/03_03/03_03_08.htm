What Are The Types of Law?


© 2011 AP Images

Laws fall into different categories. Some of the law types are:

  • Constitutional–laws relating to powers described in the Constitution
  • Civil–laws relating to the rights of private citizens
  • Criminal–laws relating to people accused of committing crimes and the punishments for those crimes
  • Military–laws relating to people who are in the armed forces and governing their behavior

The court system in the United States helps protect the rule of law for all types. Courts vary by the type of law discussed in a case. For example, state courts often separate civil and criminal courts. The U.S. military has their own courts to handle issues of military law. The U.S. Supreme CourtU.S. Supreme Court: the name for the highest court in the United States, or court of last resort, hears cases related to the U.S. Constitution can handle any type of law, but most of the cases it accepts relate to constitutional law.
