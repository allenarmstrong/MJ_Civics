Territories Text Version

(Image: Map of the United States)

Original thirteen states:

  • Virginia
  • New Jersey
  • Massachusetts
  • New Hampshire
  • Pennsylvania
  • New York
  • Maryland
  • Connecticut
  • Rhode Island
  • Delaware
  • North Carolina
  • South Carolina
  • Georgia

East and West of New York is shared territory. Land immediately to the West of Virginia and Georgia is also shared territory. Land where Florida now resides and from central U.S. to the West is Spanish territory. Land immediately South of the Great Lakes is Northwest territory during 1787.
