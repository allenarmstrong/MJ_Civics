What Do I Have to Do?

You should now be ready to define good citizenship! As you have seen, citizenship includes many important responsibilities and duties. In this activity, you will choose the responsibilities that you think are most important to good citizenship.

Steps


© 2011 Jupiterimages Corporation
  1. Review your CAP file. Make sure you have duties and responsibilities from the lesson listed in the file.
  2. Circle or highlight three duties you think are most important.
  3. Write a paragraph that explains what it means to be a good citizen. Include the three duties from Step 2 in your paragraph. There is no wrong answer, but you must use information from the lesson.
  4. Write a second paragraph to describe a time when you experienced one of the citizen responsibilities or duties, such as the example you described in your CAP file.
  5. Read the Discussion Posting Guidelines. Then, post your two paragraphs to the discussion area.
  6. Respond to at least two other students' posts. Tell them why you agree with their explanation or what you would add. Use information from the lesson in your posts.
