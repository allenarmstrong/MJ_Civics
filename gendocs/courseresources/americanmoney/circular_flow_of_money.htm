Think of the circles as water pipes. What happens when there is too much water pressure? The pipes could burst. If there is too little water pressure, the water in the pipes gets stale and the pipes may rot. Just as you would want a steady flow of water in the pipes, the Federal Reserve aims to keep a steady flow of money, resources, and goods throughout the economy.
