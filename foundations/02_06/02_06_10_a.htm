Song Template Text Version

This template will help you write a song about ratifying the Constitution. Remember to include lines that explain checks and balances, as well as the viewpoints of the Federalists and Anti-Federalists.

In each section, each line should have nearly the same number of words. Opposite lines, meaning line numbers 1 and 3, or 2 and 4, should rhyme at the end. Feel free to use this template in a creative way. You could move the bridge to another location, for example, or use your own rhyming pattern. An example verse appears at the bottom.
