Amendments Text Version

13th Amendment–Protects Americans from slavery

14th Amendment–Laws apply equally, or in the same way, for all citizens

15th Amendment–The right of citizens to vote no matter what their race or ethnicity

19th Amendment–The right of women to vote

24th Amendment–The right of citizens to vote without paying a special tax

26th Amendment–The right of citizens aged 18 and older to vote
