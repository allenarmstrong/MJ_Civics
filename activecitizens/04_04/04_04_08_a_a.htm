Rights: Answers Text Version

Instructions: Consider each fact of Gerald Gault's case. Can you determine which right was violated by each action?

  1. The accused and his family did not receive official notice of charges.

    Violation of 6th Amendment right to notification of charges
  2. Neighbor did not appear in court.

    Violation of 6th Amendment right to confront accuser in court
  3. No attorney represented him in court.

    Violation of 6th Amendment right to have an attorney
