Rights and Duties: Answers Text Version

Determine whether each item is for legal citizens only (Legal) or for all U.S. residents (All). When you are confident with your choices, select "Check Answers."

Legal:

  • Serving on a jury
  • Obtaining a U.S. passport
  • Voting in national elections
  • Working for the federal government
  • Running for federal office

All:

  • Attending school
  • Attending a city council meeting
  • Paying taxes
  • Volunteering in the community
  • Working for a privately run business, like a restaurant
  • Writing a letter to the newspaper about a local law
  • Obeying laws
