Question

Which country has the greatest amount of land area in North America?

  1. Canada
  2. Greenland
  3. Mexico
  4. United States

Feedback: Canada is the largest country in North America, followed by the United States. However, the United States is the largest country in North America by population. This means the United States has the most people living within its borders.

(Image: The world is shown with the fresh water resources per captia labeled. In cubic meters per year, South America has between 10,000 or more. Southern Africa has 10,000 or more while the more north end of Africa is less than 1000. Australia and northern Asia has 10,000 or more, while southern Asia has 1700-3999. The image has a caption stating "Fresh water is an important resource to a country. People need fresh water to survive. They cannot drink ocean water because it contains salt. Growing crops and raising farm animals requires fresh water. Fresh water is a renewable but limited resource. That means that humans could use it faster than it can renew itself if people are not careful. As you can see on this map, available fresh water varies around the world. "Per capita" means by each person, so to make this map, the amount of fresh water resources for a country was divided by its population.")

Which country of North America has the most available fresh water per capita?

  1. Canada
  2. Greenland
  3. Mexico
  4. United States

Feedback: Canada has the greatest amount of fresh water per capita in North America. It has over 10,000 cubic meters available per person, per year. However, within each country, fresh water resources can vary by location. For example, Florida has a greater challenge with fresh water access than most other U.S. states.

For most of North America, what portion of the land does natural forest cover?

  1. Less than 5%
  2. 5–14.9%
  3. 15–29.9%
  4. More than 30%

Feedback: Most of North America has between 15 and 30% natural forest cover. Imagine a checkerboard with 100 squares, and between 15 and 30 of the squares are covered with trees. That is the portion of natural forest cover in North America.

Both Canada and the United States have multiple locations of this resource, but not Mexico. What resource is it?

  1. Silver
  2. Gold
  3. Iron
  4. Coal

Feedback: The United States and Canada have multiple areas where coal is an available resource. Both countries and Mexico have silver and iron. Gold is only significant in Canada.

The countries in this table are the countries with the most crude oil reserves. Crude oil means the natural oil in the ground before humans process it into motor oil or other fuels. It is an extremely valuable resource because it is limited and humans use it in so many activities.

Which country of North America is not one of the top countries in the world with crude oil reserves?

  1. Cuba
  2. Mexico
  3. Saudi Arabia
  4. United States

Feedback: Mexico and the United States are the only countries in North America with oil reserves among the top in the world. Saudi Arabia is in Southwest Asia, not North America. The best answer is Cuba, since it is in North America but not a top country for crude oil reserves.

People have used it as money for trade and in banking. Today, it has uses ranging from medicine to glassmaking, in addition to the still-popular jewelry and decoration.

Which of the following makes a true statement?

  1. The country with the highest amount of gold reserves is in North America.
  2. No country in North America has a significant amount of gold reserves.
  3. The country with the highest amount of gold reserves is in Africa.
  4. No country in Africa has a significant amount of gold reserves.

Feedback: South Africa is the country with the highest amount of gold reserves, on the African continent. In North America, Canada and the United States have significant gold resources.
