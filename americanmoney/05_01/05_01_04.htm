Can We Have It All?

It is said that individuals and groups have endless wants. It is impossible, however, to obtain all of these wants because of limits on time, money, and other resourcesResource: a material or item that can be used to produce something new. We live in a world of scarcity, meaning limited resources. For example, imagine your list of wants includes a pair of sneakers and a cell phone. Each costs $50 and you have $65 saved. You cannot purchase both, so you must make a choice. Make a list of the options available to you. Then, check your list against the following.
