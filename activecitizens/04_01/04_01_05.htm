What Is the Debate about Offshore Drilling?

Public Domain

Now, let's examine an issue that has businesses and environmental groups at odds. Offshore drilling refers to drilling wells to obtain oil underwater. The United States claims up to 200 miles of ocean stretching out from the coastline as its territory. Many rigs already are drilling for oil within this offshore territory.
