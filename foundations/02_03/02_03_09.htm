What Do I Have to Do?

You should now be ready to create your own "Dear King George III" breakup letter! Pattern your letter like the Declaration of Independence, but be sure to write everything in your own words. In this way, you will show you understand the meaning of the declaration.


© 2011 AP Images


In your letter, be sure to include the following:

  • reasons for writing
  • beliefs about government
  • at least three specific grievances from the Declaration of Independence
  • description of two attempts to seek redress in the past and the results
  • formal declaration of the new nation

Use the Breakup Letter Template to write your letter.


Please view the Breakup Grading Rubric before starting the assignment.

  1. Complete the reading and activities for this lesson.
  2. Review your CAP file for this lesson.
  3. Complete and submit your Breakup Letter to Foundations 03 Why Is the Declaration of Independence Important?
