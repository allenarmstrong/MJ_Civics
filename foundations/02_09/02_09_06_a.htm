What Are Some Other Failed Amendment Ideas? Text Version

Child Labor–Would give Congress the power to limit, control, or possibly prohibit work for those under 18 years of age.

Term Limits for Congress–Would limit how many times a person could be elected to serve in the House of Representatives or Senate.

Balanced Budget–Would require equal amounts of income and spending of the federal government each year.

Every Vote Counts–Would end the Electoral College method of electing the president in favor of a direct national election.

Death Penalty Abolition–Would prohibit the use of the death penalty as punishment anywhere in U.S. territory.

Flag Desecration–Would make certain images and actions related to the U.S. flag (such as burning the flag in protest) against the Constitution.

Equal Opportunity to Govern–Would allow naturalized citizens at least 20 years old to run for president.

English as Official Language–Would establish English as the official language of the United States, requiring public signs and notices to be printed in English and no other language, and English-only instruction in public schools.

Right to Bear Arms–Would repeal the Second Amendment which does not specify a purpose for firearms, and state that people have the right to firearms for “hunting, sport, collecting, and personal defense.”
