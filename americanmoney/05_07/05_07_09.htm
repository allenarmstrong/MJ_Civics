ReCAP!

You should now be familiar with how credit and financial institutions affect people and the national economy.

Review your notes and the information in the lesson. Did you print or save your CAP File and place it in a safe location?

Now it's time for a game! Label these statements as true or false to check your understanding of the lesson.

Only the Federal Reserve can buy U.S. Treasury securities.

Members of the public can also buy securities, such as savings bonds.
Oops!
The correct answer is False. Members of the public can also buy securities, such as savings bonds.

Responsible use of credit can be good for the economy.

People who are careful about their use of credit can help grow the economy as well as meet their own goals.
Oops!
The correct answer is True. People who are careful about their use of credit can help grow the economy as well as meet their own goals.

The U.S. government sometimes spends money on credit.

It can and does spend money on credit, but Americans debate whether it should be allowed. Using credit carries risks even for the federal government.
Oops!
The correct answer is True. It can and does spend money on credit, but Americans debate whether it should be allowed. Using credit carries risks even for the federal government.

Using a credit card can end up costing you more money.

You will pay more money if you do not pay off the credit debt right away. Fees can add up quickly the longer you take to pay it off or if you make a late payment.
Oops!
The correct answer is True. You will pay more money if you do not pay off the credit debt right away. Fees can add up quickly the longer you take to pay it off or if you make a late payment.

The government does not owe the debt to anyone because the government just prints more money.

The people, businesses, and government agencies that buy securities own the federal debt. It is paid back to them when they cash in the securities after a certain amount of time.
Oops!
The correct answer is False. The people, businesses, and government agencies that buy securities own the federal debt. It is paid back to them when they cash in the securities after a certain amount of time.

Credit helps people start businesses, which benefits other people and businesses in the economy.

Starting a business increases exchanges of goods and services between people and other businesses.
Oops!
The correct answer is True. Starting a business increases exchanges of goods and services between people and other businesses.

Banks and businesses offer different forms of credit.

Banks offer different types of loans, installment credit, and credit cards. Businesses also offer credit cards and service credit, such as electricity service.
Oops!
The correct answer is True. Banks offer different types of loans, installment credit, and credit cards. Businesses also offer credit cards and service credit, such as electricity service.

Congress limits use of credit because it hurts the national economy.

Credit helps grow the U.S. economy when used responsibly. Congress makes rules to prevent broad consequences from poor use of credit.
Oops!
The correct answer is False. Credit helps grow the U.S. economy when used responsibly. Congress makes rules to prevent broad consequences from poor use of credit.
