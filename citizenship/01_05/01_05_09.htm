What Is the Oath of Allegiance?


© 2011 AP Images

Once immigrants complete all the other steps to become a U.S. citizen, they must swear the OathOath: a formal promise or declaration of Allegiance. They may attend a ceremony and swear the oath with other new citizens. In the oath, a person promises to do several things:

  • give up loyalty to other nations and governments
  • support and defend the U.S. Constitution and laws
  • serve the military or national government if required by law
  • make the oath without unsure or negative feelings
