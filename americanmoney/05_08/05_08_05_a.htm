  1. How does price motivate business owners?
    1. They will supply less of a product if they can charge less for it.
    2. They will supply more of a product if they can charge more for it.
    3. They will demand less of a product if they can charge more for it.
    4. They will demand more of a product if they can charge less for it.
  2. Which entrepreneur started in radio and became famous for philanthropy?
    1. Oprah Winfrey
    2. Vera Wang
    3. Joseph Unahue
    4. Ben Cohen
  3. A single business that controls the market for a good or service is called a
    1. command
    2. patent
    3. monopoly
    4. stock
  4. Adam Smith believed that the best type of economy is a ___________ economy.
    1. market
    2. command
    3. mixed
    4. traditional
  5. How do banks affect the economy?
    1. banks hurt the economy because they hold on to people's deposits.
    2. they give credit to people and businesses who in turn buy products and services.
    3. they control the debt of the federal government.
    4. banks run the businesses that borrow money from them.
  6. A mortgage payment is an example of a
    1. fixed expense
    2. variable expense
    3. credit union
    4. government tax
  7. What do individual workers supply to businesses?
    1. goods
    2. markets
    3. regulation
    4. labor
  8. Which is a similarity between household and government budgets?
    1. The expenses of both support the common good.
    2. Both types have fixed and variable expenses.
    3. Pay from working at a job is the main source of income for both.
    4. Running a deficit is the goal for both types.
  9. A disadvantage of using credit is that it
    1. Helps people meet their goals sooner
    2. May assist entrepreneurs to expand their businesses
    3. Is useful in an emergency such as a natural disaster
    4. Can lead to people spending money they don't have
  10. Before the Federal Reserve can increase sales of federal bonds,
    1. the U.S. Treasury must give its permission
    2. individual banks must increase their interest rates on loans
    3. congress must approve raising the deficit of the federal budget
    4. the president must adjust the budget to decrease spending
  11. Imagine your favorite cereal is made of corn, and most of this year's corn crop was destroyed by wildfires. The supply of your favorite cereal decreases. What will likely happen?
    1. The price of your favorite cereal will increase.
    2. The price of your favorite cereal will decrease.
    3. The demand for your favorite cereal will increase.
    4. The demand for your favorite cereal will decrease.
  12. You can either study for your civics exam or watch television. If you choose the TV, your opportunity cost is the
    1. time and experience of studying for your exam and possibly earning a higher grade
    2. time and experience of watching TV and possibly talking to your friends about the shows
    3. the time you would spend talking on the phone to your friends
    4. the money you spend on electric and cable service for the TV
