You should now be familiar with how geography has influenced the people and standard of living in North America. GDP per capita is a data figure that tells us the value of a country's goods and services by each person living there. It alone does not tell us much about standard of living. We need to study other data and compare it with other countries. The countries of North America work together to improve standard of living in themselves and each other.
