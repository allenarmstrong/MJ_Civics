People move for many reasons, and varied factors affect their choices. Often when people move, they look for a place that has a better standard of living. They hope for a more comfortable lifestyle. Geography has an important role in shaping an area's standard of living. Let's learn more about geography and its affect on a country.


Use this CAP File to make notes during this lesson.







Objectives

After completing this lesson, you will be able to:

  • explain how geography has influenced settlement, trade, and governments in North America
  • compare and contrast the standard of living in various countries today to that of the United States using gross domestic product (GDP)
