How Can I Learn More?

You can learn a lot about service in your community by interviewing someone who volunteers. Think about people you already know. Someone you know is likely a volunteer through at least one of the types of service. You will be conducting an interview for this lesson's assessment.

Try this activity to learn How to Conduct an Interview.


Have you begun the service project you are required to complete? This interview can be part of the "Plan!" step of your service project. Find someone to speak with who volunteers in an activity of interest to you.
