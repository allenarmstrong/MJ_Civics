How Do Super Citizens Support the Common Good?


© 2011 AP Images

Direct—volunteering at an animal shelter, cleaning a park, building a playground, tutoring a child, serving food at a homeless shelter, growing endangered plant species

Indirect—collecting money or pet food for the animal shelter, creating a budget for the new playground, gathering books to send to a needy school, collecting canned food items for the homeless shelter, donating garbage bags to a beach cleanup

Advocacy—creating a website for the animal shelter, calling people to get volunteers to build the playground, writing a letter to the newspaper about the importance of reading to young children, sending an email to the city council about the needs of the homeless shelter
