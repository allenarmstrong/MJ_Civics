Image of a political spectrum. The base of the spectrum is a bold line that runs horizontally. There is an arrow pointing toward the left end of the spectrum labeled "to the left". Below the arrow is a blank like for the student to label the ideology. The center of the spectrum is labeled "center" and the ideology is identified as "Moderate". There is an arrow pointing toward the right end of the spectrum labeled "to the right". Below the arrow is a blank line for the student to label the ideology. Under the spectrum on the left, there is a blank line for students to identify the political party associated with the left end of the spectrum. Under the spectrum on the right, there is a blank line for students to identify the political party associated with the right end of the spectrum.
