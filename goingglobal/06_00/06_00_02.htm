Going Global is all about how the United States relates to other countries around the world. In this unit, you will learn about the governments of other countries, why and how countries interact, and how they work together to improve life for all.

Keep track of your learning using the CAP file for each lesson. Print your CAP files to take notes. Be sure to place them together in a safe location. Taking notes will help you prepare for assessments, including the Discussion-Based Assessment, and exams.

Does your city have a "sister city?" In the United States, a sister city is a foreign city whose local government has partnered officially with a U.S. city. They agree to a long-term relationship of culture, friendship, and business. Tallahassee, Florida has five sister cities!
