What content did you learn? 40 points

All of the following are present:
  • purpose of the bill or law
  • history of the bill or law
  • effects on other levels of government and people in general
  • different viewpoints on the bill or law
  • student viewpoint on the bill or law
 30 points

No more than one of the following is missing:
  • purpose of the bill or law
  • history of the bill or law
  • effects on other levels of government and people in general
  • different viewpoints on the bill or law
  • student viewpoint on the bill or law
 20 points

No more than two of the following are missing:
  • purpose of the bill or law
  • history of the bill or law
  • effects on other levels of government and people in general
  • different viewpoints on the bill or law
  • student viewpoint on the bill or law

No more than two of the following are missing:
  • accurate details
