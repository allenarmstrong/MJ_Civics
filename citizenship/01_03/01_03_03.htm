What are GPS and GIS?

Have you ever seen or made a treasure map? Maps may not lead you to a chest full of riches, but they can lead you to information worth learning. You can focus on a single map point, like a latitude and longitude coordinate. You can also view information on maps that apply to a wide area of points.

The Global Positioning System, or GPSGlobal Positioning System (GPS): uses satellite technology developed for the military to locate a precise coordinate point for someone or something, tells us how to find a single point on Earth. It is like an electronic treasure map. The Global Information System, or GISGlobal Information Systems (GIS): sets of descriptive or scientific data for a place, tells us information about that point. Learn more about GPS, GIS, and special types of maps. Try this Map Activity to practice the skills needed for success in this lesson.
